About PawsHaven

Our mission, our team, and the story behind our shelter.

Our Mission

Founded in 2018, PawsHaven started as a small foster network run by Dr. Sarah Mitchell and a team of dedicated volunteers. Today, we operate a full-service veterinary clinic and rescue shelter serving over 500 animals each year.

Every animal that enters our facility receives comprehensive medical care, behavioral assessment, and individualized attention. We never euthanize for space — every life matters.

Our state-of-the-art clinic provides vaccinations, spay/neuter surgeries, dental care, emergency treatment, and ongoing medical management for animals with chronic conditions.
